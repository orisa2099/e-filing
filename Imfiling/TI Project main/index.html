file documents DIRECTLY to the courts

Our goal with iM-filing is to save lawyers the stress and time involved in submitting documents for civil processes manually to the Nigerian courts.

iM-Filing is a product of Team impact, not affilated with any law firm. We are an online Nigerian Certified Third Party Vendor and specialize in automating the filing of court processes.

Access a lawyer or law firm around you

iM-filing also seeks to connect members of the general public to lawyers and law firms around them. We facilitate the easy access of people to reputable and reliable lawyers who can bring an action in the court of law on their behalf
